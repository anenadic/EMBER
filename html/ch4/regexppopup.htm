Example Patterns


CC.[LF].[DEN][LI].C
In this example, C must be present at positions 1,2 and 9; "." indicates that any residue is allowed; square brackets denote a range of residues allowed at a position (e.g., [DEN] indicates that D, E or N may occur). Please note, patterns must be at least 3 residues long (expect a lot of output if they are!) and the more permissive the expression (e.g. many ".") the less specific it will be.
